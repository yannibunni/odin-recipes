Lasagna

The best Lasagna you've ever tasted!

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and l ots of mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
Steps
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
