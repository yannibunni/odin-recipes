Cabonara

Discover how to make superb spaghetti carbonara. This cheesy pasta dish is an Italian favourite and with the right technique, you can make it perfect every time

Ingredients
  • Pancetta
  • Pecorino cheese
  • Parmeasan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50g unsalted butter
Steps
  1. Put a large saucepan of water on boil
  2. Chop the pancetta
  3. Beat 3 large eggs
  4. Add 1 tsp salt to the boiling water
  5. Squash 2 peeled garlic cloves
  6. Mix cheese in with the eggs
